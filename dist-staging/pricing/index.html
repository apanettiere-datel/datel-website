Cloud SWEET features

Everything teams need to monitor calls, manage staffing, and share insights

Cloud SWEET combines real-time visibility, historical reporting, and operational control in one managed platform.

Completely hosted
Launch without extra hardware or software and keep implementation overhead low.
Real-time call activity visibility
Monitor volume, distribution, and team activity as calls complete.
Instant call reporting
Access summary and detailed call reporting immediately after each interaction.
Drill-down analytics
Move from KPI-level dashboards to event-level call detail in a few clicks.
Scheduled and on-demand reports
Automate recurring report delivery and run ad hoc analysis whenever needed.
Export-ready output
Share insights quickly with PDF and Excel exports for leadership and operations teams.
Call accounting insight
Track trends, peak periods, and duration behavior to improve staffing and control costs.
Productivity and alerting tools
Use thresholds and misuse alerts to surface issues early and maintain service quality.
